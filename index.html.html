I Am What God Says I AM

Vision

To become the world's leading platform for affirmations and poverty eradication, empowering millions globally.

Mission

Empowering individuals to live fearlessly and confidently through affirmations, while fostering a deeper connection with God through prayer, word study, and worship. We also aim to cultivate a global network of philanthropists while raising kingdom Financiers who will expand God's kingdom by spreading love, hope, and kindness through their generosity both locally and internationally.

Purpose

To transform lives worldwide by sharing the power of affirmations, God's word, and philanthropy, inspiring positive change and spreading love, hope, and kindness.

Programs

  • Midnight prayers every Friday
  • Worship session on the first Saturday of every month
  • Charity outreaches every year

DAME has two arms: The Affirmation Part and the Humanitarian/Philanthropic/NGO Part.

Partnership Vision

Creating a global household community of like-minded individuals and organizations to create a positive impact in the lives of those in need, fostering a culture of compassion, hope, and integrity.

Partnership Mission

To make a tangible difference in the lives of individuals and communities through supportive partnerships, empowerment, and collective action, guided by the principles of compassion, hope, empowerment, and integrity.

Core Values

  • Compassion: Empathy and kindness in all our interactions.
  • Hope: Uplifting others and fostering promise for a brighter future.
  • Empowerment: Equipping individuals with support and resources to thrive.
  • Integrity: Transparency, honesty, and accountability in all actions.

Becoming a Partner

  • Monthly Commitment: Minimum of N10,000 monthly.
  • Additional Contributions:
    • Material support (food, clothing, toiletries)
    • Financial support (donations, sponsorships)
    • Connections (linking us with partners or beneficiaries)
    • Logistics (transport, storage, equipment)
    • Location support (venues for outreach)
  • Volunteering: Join our outreaches with your time, skills, and support.

We deeply appreciate every sacrifice made to be part of DAME. May God bless and reward your generosity and commitment.
